MEAMA PRMTR
Product Specification  v2.0
Commercial CRM Platform — E-Commerce + Brand Stores

Version
2.0 — merged from NextGen spec + field-level spec
Date
June 2026
Status
Final draft — for data team and engineering
Scope
Meama Georgia: Brand Store + Ecommerce + Call — plus Meama Corner (Facebook group) as separate channel
Channels excluded
Vending, B2B, Collect — isolated in data layer, not surfaced in platform
Margin floor
40% gross margin minimum enforced on all promotion calculations
Churn definition
No order in ≥ 3 calendar months
At-risk threshold
No order in 45–89 days
Capsule loyalists rule
Loyalty + explorer segment: zero discount offers. Use early access instead.
Max discount allowed
25% — hard cap across all campaign types

What we are building — and why
Meama operates across e-commerce, brand stores, vending, and B2B with no unified view of its customers, products, or campaigns. Retention and upsell decisions are made manually, based on experience rather than data.

Module
Problem it solves
Customer 360
No unified behavioural profile exists per customer
Product Intelligence
No view of what sells with what, or why
Campaign Intelligence
Campaigns run without prediction or ROI measurement
Command Overview
No single executive health view across all channels
Alerts
No early warning system for stock, churn, or margin risk
Reports
Reporting is manual, ad hoc, and retrospective

CALL CENTER AUTOMATED REPORTS
Two automated call reports are pre-loaded in the platform each day, giving Call Center agents a prioritised queue with all relevant customer data and the recommended offer pre-selected. Agents do not need to look up any information manually.

REPORT 1 — MACHINE BUYER FOLLOW-UP CALL
FIELD
DETAIL
Purpose
Onboard new machine owners; convert to active capsule buyers before first-30-day drop-off window
Trigger
Customer order contains a machine SKU (any format: cap37 / cap51 / tea / mix)
Timing
Call attempted within 24 hours of confirmed delivery (courier status = Delivered)
Priority
HIGH — appears at the top of the daily call queue
SLA
If unreached on Day 1, retry Day 2 and Day 3; log outcome after each attempt
Agent data shown
Customer name, machine model, delivery date, capsule format compatibility, top-3 recommended capsule SKUs (pre-matched to machine format), total capsule orders to date
Recommended script goal
Confirm machine is working; offer to send a starter capsule pack; explain subscription / auto-replenishment options
Offer attached
Starter capsule bundle matched to machine format at introductory price (configured in Promotions module)
Outcome tags
Reached: Interested / Reached: Not interested / No answer / Wrong number / Scheduled callback
Auto-close rule
Report line closes when first capsule order is placed or after 3 failed attempts

Report line closes when next order is placed or customer is flagged as Opted Out
